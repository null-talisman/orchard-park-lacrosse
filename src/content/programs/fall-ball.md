Fall Ball is a low-key opportunity to work on skills and have fun before the winter season.

## Program Details

- **Season**: September - October 2026
- **Practices**: 1x per week
- **Games**: Scrimmages on weekends
- **Location**: Orchard Park fields

## Focus Areas

- Fundamental skill development
- Position-specific training
- Team building
- Preparing for winter/spring seasons

Great for new players looking to try lacrosse or returning players wanting to stay sharp!
