Our flagship program! Boys Spring Lacrosse runs from mid-March through May and offers competitive play for all skill levels.

## Program Details

- **Season**: March 15 - May 30, 2026
- **Practices**: 2x per week (weekday evenings)
- **Games**: Saturday mornings
- **Location**: Orchard Park High School Fields

## Age Divisions

- **U8 (K-2nd)**: Introduction to lacrosse fundamentals
- **U10 (3rd-4th)**: Skill development and modified games
- **U12 (5th-6th)**: Full-field play and league competition
- **U14 (7th-8th)**: Advanced skills and travel opportunities
- **High School**: JV and Varsity teams

## What's Included

- Team jersey and shorts
- Professional coaching
- League registration
- Insurance coverage
- End of season awards

## Equipment Needed

Players must provide their own helmet, gloves, and stick. We have loaner equipment available for new players.
