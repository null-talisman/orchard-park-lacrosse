As we prepare for our spring season, we're looking for dedicated volunteers to join our coaching staff.

## What We're Looking For

- Positive attitude and enthusiasm for working with kids
- Commitment to practices (2x per week) and games
- Background check required (we cover the cost)
- Prior lacrosse experience helpful but not required

## What You'll Get

- Free coaching clinic and certification
- OP Lacrosse coaching gear
- The rewarding experience of mentoring young athletes

## How to Apply

Fill out our [coaching application](/coaching-application) or email us at coaches@oplax.org.
